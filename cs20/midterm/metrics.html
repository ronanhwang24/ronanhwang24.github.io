What EarlyBird Measures

Track Every Step of Your Health & Fitness Journey

From sleep to stress, and steps to recovery, EarlyBird captures everything to coach you towards your best performance.

What do we track?

  • Sleep quality:
             We give you sleep quality information based on your time in deep sleep, light sleep,
    REM sleep, and awake time
  • Heart Rate Variability:
             A key indicator of recovery and stress levels, showing how well your body adapts to daily challenges.
  • Stress Levels:
             We help you identify when your body is under stress and how to manage it for better performance.
  • Recovery:
             Get personalized recommendations to help you rest and recover based on your exertion levels.

    • By understanding the interplay between sleep, stress, recovery, and physical exertion, EarlyBird empowers you to make smarter decisions about your health and fitness.
