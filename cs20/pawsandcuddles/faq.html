FAQ

  1. What services do you offer?

    2. We provide a range of pet sitting services, including daily visits, overnight stays, dog walking, feeding, playtime, and administering medication if needed. We also offer customized care based on your pet’s specific needs.

  2. How do I book a pet sitting service?

    3. Booking is easy! You can schedule a service by visiting our website, calling us, or sending an email. We recommend booking in advance, especially during holidays or peak seasons.

  3. Will I meet my pet sitter before the service?

    4. Yes! We offer a free meet-and-greet session before your first booking. This allows you to introduce your pet to the sitter, discuss any special instructions, and ensure a comfortable experience for everyone.

  4. How do you ensure my pet’s safety?

    5. Our pet sitters are trained professionals who prioritize your pet’s well-being. We follow your instructions carefully, provide regular updates, and ensure a secure environment. Additionally, all sitters are insured and background-checked.

  5. Can you care for pets with special needs?

    6. Absolutely! We have experience caring for pets with medical conditions, senior pets, and those requiring special attention. Please inform us about any specific needs during booking so we can provide the best care.

  6. What should I provide for my pet during the service?

    7. Please provide food, treats, a leash, any medications, and anything else your pet may need. If your pet has a favorite toy or blanket, we encourage you to leave it with them for added comfort.

  7. How will I receive updates about my pet?

    8. We provide regular updates through text, email, or our mobile app. This may include photos, videos, and a summary of your pet’s activities during our visit.

  8. What happens in case of an emergency?

    9. In case of an emergency, we will contact you immediately. If necessary, we will take your pet to the nearest veterinary clinic and follow any instructions you provide in your pet’s care profile.

  9. What is your cancellation policy?

    10. We understand that plans can change. Cancellations made at least 24 hours in advance are eligible for a full refund. Last-minute cancellations may be subject to a small fee.

  10. Do you offer services for all types of pets?

    11. Yes! While we primarily care for dogs and cats, we also provide services for birds, rabbits, and other small animals. If you have an exotic pet, contact us, and we’ll discuss how we can accommodate their needs.
